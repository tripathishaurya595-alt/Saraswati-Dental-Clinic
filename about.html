Our Story

Caring for Lucknow's Smiles Since 2013

Saraswati Dental Clinic was founded by Dr. Devesh Mishra in 2013 with a single purpose: to make quality dental care truly accessible to families across Lucknow — without the fear, the jargon, or the surprise bills.

Over more than a decade, we've grown from a small practice in Hazratganj into a trusted multi-specialty clinic, caring for patients ranging from toddlers on their first visit to grandparents seeking restorative work. The same values that shaped us on day one still guide every treatment we deliver.

We believe dentistry works best when it feels like a conversation — where you understand exactly what's happening, why it's needed, and what it'll cost before anything begins.

  • Personalised care, never one-size-fits-all
  • Honest, transparent treatment plans
  • No referrals needed — most specialities in-house
  • Welcoming environment for adults and children alike
